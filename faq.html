What is included in the price of a shared room?

All utilities are included, which includes electricity, water, sewer, cable TV, internet service and trash removal. All of our shared rooms come equipped with 2 beds, 2 mattresses, 2 desks with desk chairs, 2 spacious closets, a private bathroom with a sink, shower curtain, trash can, microwave and mini-refrigerator.

What is included in the price of a private room?

All utilities are included, which includes electricity, water, sewer, cable TV, internet service and trash removal. All of our private rooms come equipped with a bed, mattress, desk with desk chair, a spacious closet, a private bathroom with a sink, shower curtain, trash can, microwaveand mini-refrigerator.

Do I have to pay for Internet?

No. Every room has its own Internet access point, so all you have to do is bring a router (to give you your own Wi-Fi connection), an Ethernet cable (connect your router directly into our main student network), and your laptop!
